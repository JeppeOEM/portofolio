Nemskat

Visit the site try the app

A presentation homepage and prototype of an accounting app targeting young people with part time jobs.
The app offers an easy way to keep track of your tax reporting by uploading paychecks directly from your camera roll
The app then tells you what to write in your tax report.
This project consisted of a fully developed buisness proposal.
First thing was to know our users, so we interviewed young people about tax reporting and did usability tests of the app.
Many young people dont like the subject of tax so we developed a social media concept around the charecter "skattefar" to make the topic more fun
This can be seen on our instagram https://www.instagram.com/nemskatnu/
My work consisted in coding the homepage, developing the UI tests and the design of social media content and strategy, my two instagram posts is seen above.
Furthermore I could use my knowledge of API's and hosting to resarch the cost of the project as a buisness.
